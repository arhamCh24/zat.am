Language / Script Demo

This page shows how your saved language/script preference and bilingual toggle can change how Sanskrit text is displayed using Sanscript.js.

The underlying text is stored once in a romanization scheme (Harvard-Kyoto) and then transliterated into different scripts.

Your current setting renders the sample verse as:

dharmakSetre kurukSetre

To change how this looks, go back to the dashboard and adjust your Language & Script settings.
